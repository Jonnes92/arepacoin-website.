About Arepacoin

Arepacoin is more than a cryptocurrency; it is a movement that blends culture, food, and community support. Inspired by the Venezuelan arepa, we aim to connect and uplift Latin Americans across the globe.

Mission

To celebrate and preserve Venezuelan culture, support Latin communities worldwide, and provide resources for emigrants and those in need.

Vision

To be the leading cultural cryptocurrency that empowers communities and fosters solidarity across borders.

Traditional Games

Celebrate the joy of traditional childhood games that brought communities together. Learn their history and how to play them!

Metras (Marbles)

Metras, or marbles, is a classic game where players aim to hit opponents' marbles out of a circle. A game of skill and strategy loved by kids for generations.

Trompo (Spinning Top)

The trompo is a spinning top game where players compete to keep their top spinning the longest or perform tricks.

Papagallos (Kites)

Papagallos, or kites, are colorful creations flown high in the sky, often during festive gatherings. A symbol of freedom and creativity.

Other Group Games

Explore games like escondite (hide and seek), la cuerda (jump rope), and other classics that brought kids together in joyful play.
